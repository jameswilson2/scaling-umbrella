Holiday home broadband

Superfast and ultrafast broadband services to help you make the most of your time there.

Our services work just like a home broadband service and will enable you to:

  • Stream programs
  • Make video calls
  • Game online
  • Use social media
  • Monitor your holiday home with CCTV
  • Control your home remotely (heating, doorbell etc.)

Best of all, you can suspend your services when you’re not there.

At each holiday park we provide our service using a combination of fibre and wireless technology. The speeds and data levels we are able to deliver is dependent on the nature of connectivity available at the park. This explains why our prices start from £25 per month for unlimited broadband. We apply fair usage data caps to our services to ensure that everyone gets a good service.
