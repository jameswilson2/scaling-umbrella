Wi-Fi for Holiday Lets

We provide easy to use, remotely managed Wi-Fi management systems (hotspots) for landlords.  Whether you own one property or one hundred, we can help you to manage Wi-Fi access for your short term lets.

There are three important considerations when providing Wi-Fi:

  • Protecting yourself from unlawful use of the internet access you provide (legally the account holder is responsible)
  • Managing costs in relation to excess usage charges for data
  • Generating revenue

Our remotely managed Wi-Fi hotspot solution gives landlords control of the Wi-Fi by:

  • Enabling landlords to change passwords remotely and thereby set unique passwords for each letting.
  • Set data limits remotely
  • Set speed restrictions remotely
  • Generate revenue

To find out more about how to manage Wi-Fi access for your holiday lets more efficiently please call our sales team now.  We’ll be happy to share our knowledge and expertise with you.
