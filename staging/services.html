Additional services

We design and build network solutions tailored to your requirements.

Wi-Fi solutions for all locations

We specialise in wireless technologies. It has been our business for over 20 years and we use our experience and in-depth knowledge to provide effective Wi-Fi solutions for all locations including, hotels, holiday parks, student accommodation, outdoor venues and many more.

Advanced telephony

Today’s phone systems enable more productivity with smart and helpful features and they are cheaper than old fashioned phone systems. We deploy 3CX as our carrier class phone system. 3CX is feature rich and can be deployed in specialised configurations such as working from home, call centre, hotel or office to suit your needs. 3CX can be used on deskphones, mobile apps and computers, it is extremely versatile and can work from anywhere in the world.

Bespoke Connectivity

Our origin and expertise are in providing connectivity to rural areas. With 20 years of experience, we pride ourselves in finding solutions.

Our highly skilled Cisco certified network engineers can apply our technology to deliver bespoke solutions for your business. We can provide ultrafast connectivity, inter-site links and wide area networks tailored to your needs.

If you have a requirement for internet connectivity, whether a temporary requirement (like a concert or exhibition) or a permanent requirement, give us a call.

Hosting solutions

As a full service ISP we provide supported hosting services for those customers who want a ‘one stop shop’. Give us a call if you are interested in website hosting, server hosting and email hosting.
